Welcome to the Bank of High Tides online banking!

With the Bank of High Tides online banking application, you have several options. To make your selection click the button below and enter a valid selection:

  • Enter D to make a DEPOSIT.
  • Enter W to make a WITHDRAWAL.
  • Enter B to view your BALANCE.
  • Enter Q to close (QUIT) the application. NOTE: you will have to QUIT the application in order for it to close.
